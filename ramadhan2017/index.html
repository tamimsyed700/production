Beneficial supplication to make at the time of breaking the fast.

Supplication is the essence of worship[Tirmidhi]. “And your Lord says, “Call upon Me: I will respond to you.” Indeed, those who disdain My worship will enter Hell [rendered] contemptible.” [40:60]

Here are our favorites supplication that we think every Muslim should ask to Allah Almighty at the time of breaking the fast on top of their own supplication. The supplication we have compiled involves asking Allah for protection in this world and in the Hereafter and guidance and forgiveness in this world and the Hereafter.

We hope people find it useful and share with their friends and family. That being said, without wasting the time let's head down to the list of supplication below with importance in no particular order. Each section is split into 3. One being the Arabic dua, 2nd for English transliteration and the 3 for explanation in English.

1. Seeking protection against deprivation of Allah’s bounties and withdrawal of Allah’s security

Arabic dua Want to read in English?
English transliteration Want to read in Arabic?
Explanation of Dua.

O Allah, I seek refuge in you from being deprived of your bounties, from losing your security, from your sudden revenge, and from all your dismay.

Reference : Sahih Muslim # 2739

2. Seeking protection against trial of life and death and protection against Ad-Dajjal

Arabic dua Want to read in English?
English transliteration Want to read in Arabic?
Explanation of Dua.

O Allah, I seek refuge in you from the affliction of the torment of the Hell fire, from the affliction of the torment in the grave, from the trial of life and death and from the evil of the affliction of Al Maseeh Ad Dajjal (Anti-Christ).

Reference : Sahih Al Bukhari # 1377

3. Seeking protection against affliction of wealth,poverty and Hell fire and torment of the grave

Arabic dua Want to read in English?
English transliteration Want to read in Arabic?
Explanation of Dua.

O Allah, I seek refuge in you from the affliction of the HellFire and its torment, from the affliction of the grave and its torment, and from the affliction of wealth and from the affliction of poverty.

Reference : Sahih Al Bukhari # 6377

4. Seeking goodness in all affairs

Arabic dua Want to read in English?
English transliteration Want to read in Arabic?
Explanation of Dua.

O Allah, It is your Mercy I am seeking. So let me not give in to myself even for the span of an eyes wink. Make good all my affairs. There is no god but Allah

Reference : Sunan Abu Dawood # 5090

5. Seeking protection against poverty and getting into disbelief

Arabic dua Want to read in English?
English transliteration Want to read in Arabic?
Explanation of Dua.

O Allah, I seek refuge in you from disbelief (in You), poverty and torment of the grave

Reference : Sunan An Nasai # 5464

6. Seeking protection against satan’s temptation and other calamities

Arabic dua Want to read in English?
English transliteration Want to read in Arabic?
Explanation of Dua.

O Allah, I seek refuge in you from demolitions. I seek refuge in You from falling down from high places.I seek refuge in You from drowning,burning and old age. I seek refuge in You from Satan’s temptations at death

Reference : Sunan Abu Dawood # 1552

7. Comprehensive dua for health, seeking forgiveness, protection of family and wealth as well as security.

Arabic dua Want to read in English?
English transliteration Want to read in Arabic?
Explanation of Dua.

O Allah, I ask you for health in this world and in the Hereafter. O Allah, I ask you for forgiveness, for soundness of faith and for security in this world and safety of my family and of my wealth. O Allah, keep my awraat (body parts. secrets.etc., that are forbidden to be revealed to others) concealed, and protect me from being terrorized.O Allah, protect me from the front and the back, from my right and my left and from above, and I seek refuge in your Magnificence from being swallowed (by the Earth) from beneath.

Reference : Sunan Abu Dawood # 5074
